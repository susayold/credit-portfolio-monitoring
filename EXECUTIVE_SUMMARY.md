# Executive Summary

## Decision supported

Identify where credit risk is deteriorating, distinguish observed portfolio movement from synthetic controls testing, and convert breaches into accountable management action.

## Headline evidence

- 1.35M historical accepted/booked accounts in the observed monitoring base.
- Maturity-safe outcomes, concentration, discrimination, calibration, stability, policy, ECL and KRI controls.
- Seven-view authoritative browser dashboard.
- Analytical validation 131/131, UAT 56/56, SIT 25/25, negative tests 25/25, Excel reconciliation 13/13 and dashboard checks 25/25.

## Employer value

The project demonstrates the ability to move from metrics to management decisions: isolate the source of deterioration, quantify policy/capacity trade-offs, assign owners and preserve traceable evidence.

## Boundary

Observed conclusions relate to historical matured accepted/booked accounts. Delinquency, roll-rate, cure and vintage servicing are synthetic controls-testing evidence. ECL is proxy-based and policy impact is a historical routing simulation.
