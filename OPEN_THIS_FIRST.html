Employer value

What this project proves

The project is designed around the decisions a Portfolio Risk or Risk Analytics team must make, not around isolated charts.

Portfolio diagnosis

Connect current/prior movement, exposure, grade concentration, maturity and observed bad rate into a defensible risk view.

Model and policy oversight

Monitor AUC, KS, calibration and fixed-bin PSI, then quantify cutoff trade-offs without reopening the frozen scorecard.

Governed management action

Translate breaches into owner, investigation, quantified remediation, acceptance criteria and objective closure evidence.

Evidence architecture

From frozen inputs to controlled decisions

Every output has a defined evidence status and a traceable path back to source data, calculation and validation.

Claim discipline

Observed, synthetic and proxy evidence stay separate

The dashboard never upgrades an assumption or controls-test layer into an observed production claim.
